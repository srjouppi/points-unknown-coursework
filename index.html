POINTS UNKNOWN

A site to host assignments and final deliverables for the Points Unknown class at Columbia University's Graduate School of Journalism.

About the Author

Some psychologists cite low reimbursement rates as a reason they don't enroll with Medicaid. I scraped Psychology Today's provider directory to determine how much private therapists are charging in New York City.

I visualized the results with a combination of handmade illustrations, ai2html and d3 to create this scrollytelling.
