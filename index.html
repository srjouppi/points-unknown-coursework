About the Author

I'm Shelby Jouppi, a data journalist finishing up my master's degree at Columbia University in New York, NY. Before this I was a freelance podcast producer and a public radio reporter at Detroit's NPR station. As a mama to a child with special health needs, I am deeply passionate about reporting on health care accessibility and filling information gaps for families.
